WELCOME TO MKP IT SERVICES

MKP IT Services is a leading training, job and staffing services provider in Nagpur. We have over a decade of presence now. Training and Internship: We conduct high end training and provide intership program to freshers to strengthen them with corporate required skills.

Campus Placement Services: We conduct placements services for freshers, skilled and semi-skilled candidates.

Lateral Placement Services: Placement services for junior, mid level and senior level recruitment.

Job fairs at College Level: Conduct job fairs for aspiring students at college level.

Campus Recruitment Training: We conduct workshops on soft skills, written skills, mock interview preparations and training, personality development training.

We have had a successful track record of meeting the vast manpower requirements of varied industries.
